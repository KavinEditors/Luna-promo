Features

L

Language

Luna communicates fluently in any language — from English and Arabic to Japanese and beyond. It handles complex multilingual queries, translates content on the fly, and even solves mathematical equations and expressions, making it a universal thinking partner regardless of your native tongue.

UI

A clean, polished, and fully customisable interface designed to feel native to your browser. Luna's UI adapts to your workflow — minimal when you need focus, rich when you need depth. Every interaction is smooth, intentional, and built with pixel-perfect attention to detail.

U
N

Neural Network

Beneath the surface, Luna runs on a powerful neural backbone powered by the Groq API — supporting high-performance OpenAI-compatible and leading LLM models. It processes language at exceptional speed, handles multi-modal inputs, and can accurately describe and interpret images, charts, and visual content in real time.

Agent

Luna acts as your intelligent browser agent — proactively analysing the websites you visit, extracting structured insights from PDF documents, and answering questions about content you're actively viewing. It's user-friendly by design, requiring no setup or technical knowledge, just open it and start exploring.
